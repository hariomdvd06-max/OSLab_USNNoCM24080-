The 4 Coffman Conditions

🔒

Mutual Exclusion

The intersection bounds can only be occupied by one car's specific path at a time. Sharing is physically impossible.

⚓

Hold & Wait

Cars keep their current position in the intersection while waiting for the next segment to become free.

⛔

No Preemption

We cannot physically force cars to back up and yield their acquired space to another car.

🔄

Circular Wait

North waits for West, West waits for South, South waits for East, East waits for North. Result: a locked loop.
